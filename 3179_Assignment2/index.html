Country And Continent Profile Statistics

A Looking at different statistics For every Country/Continent.

Employment

Firstly, We look at the Employment section for every region across the world. There is some very interesting data shown within these graphs that show which economic sector plays the highest factor in providing jobs and income for its population. Countries in different regions such as Eastern Africa and Western Africa provide more job opportunities in the agriculture sector than Oceania while it is vice versa for services sector, this plays into the fact of the differences between a developed country and an underdeveloped country.

Education

Next, the Education section also has an important role of a country's future and its economic growth. By looking at the current statistics which filtered each country into different continent, there is some insightful data that can be extracted from these visualisations.

International Trade

Having good diplomatic relations with many different countries would bring many benefits to a country, including imports of foreign goods and exports of local goods. For this section, we would look into the exports and imports of each country. From the data that is shown, we can gather that countries which major in manufacturing would have a high amount of trade buisness in exporting goods while countries with limited natural resources would have higher imports.

Health

Lastly, we look at the Health section for every country. Moreover, we are looking at few factors that would influence the likelihood of a person's health on each country, where the factors include CO2 emissions around the globe, life expectancy at birth for male and female and infant mortality rate.
